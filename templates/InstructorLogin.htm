Welcome to the Research Methods Dojo! Training at the Dojo is simple:

1. Create an Instructor Account. This will let you log in to see your students' progress in the RM Dojo modules.

2. Create a Course. Once you have created an account, you will be taken to a menu page where you may add a course. Just choose a name for the course and select the term.

3. Distribute the Course Code. The data page for each course will display a unique identification number for the course. Your students will need this number to register for your course in the Research Methods Dojo.
